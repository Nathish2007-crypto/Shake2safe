About Shake2Save

Our solution is Shake2Save, a Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to make women's safety faster, simpler, and more accessible.

Key Highlights:

  • Lightweight PWA: Works directly from the browser and can be added to the home screen â€” no installation barrier.
  • Live Safety Map: Shows safe/unsafe zones based on crowdsourced reports and public data.
  • Quick Reporting: Allows users to mark unsafe areas with a single tap.
  • Shake-to-Alert (Unique Feature): Just shake the phone to instantly send your live location and an SOS message.
  • One-Tap SOS Button: A large, visible emergency button is always accessible for manual triggering.
